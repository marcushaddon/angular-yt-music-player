What is this mycrate.io?

mycrate.io is a place to store, organize, and share rare and obscure music you find on Youtube. Albums uploaded to Youtube as single videos can be split into tracks that can then be shared and added to playlists individually.

This app is also a work in progress, and we have many more features planned. You can check out a list of planned features here. If there's something we haven't thought of, or if something doesn't work right, let us know here!
